🚨 Problem Statement
❌ Traditional Challenges

Employee data is often stored in Excel sheets or paper records

This leads to inefficiencies such as:

🔍 Difficulty in searching employee records

✏️ Complexity in updating information

🏢 Poor tracking of roles and departments

⚠️ Risks Involved

📉 High chances of data loss

❌ Increased human errors

🔁 Duplicate entries due to lack of centralized system
